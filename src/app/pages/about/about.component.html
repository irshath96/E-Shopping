OUR STORY

In year 2022, the visionary Mr. Milan & Mr. Brijesh started export business which registered as “Baldha Export”. After so many ups and downs Baldha Export got its first breakthrough while visiting Gulf. Then company started its operation with agriculture products to support farmers. Company started dealings in Guar Gum, Dehydrated Garlic & onion, mangoes, bananas, groundnuts and whatnot.

Later on, in 2017 company started one another vertical named Limpio Chem LLP, which is now parent company of Baldha Export. company added more range of products like chemical, surgical equipment, machinery, Kitchen cutlery, construction materials, textile items, etc., who want to export their products. Further Limpio Chem LLP associated with India’s top 15 FMCG brands and started supplying FMCG products to overseas super markets.
